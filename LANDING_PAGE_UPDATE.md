# Landing Page Update - Dual Portal Access

## Changes Made

### Navigation Bar
**Before:** Single "Login" button
**After:** Two buttons
- **"Patient Login"** (Primary - Green) → `/patient-login`
- **"Staff"** (Secondary - Gray) → `/login`

### Hero Section
**Before:** "Professional Health Management" (medical staff focused)
**After:** "Your Health, Simplified" (inclusive for both)

**Subtitle Updated:**
- Now mentions both patients and healthcare professionals
- Emphasizes personal health tracking AND professional care

**CTA Buttons:**
- **"Patient Portal"** (Primary - Green gradient) → `/patient-login`
- **"Medical Staff Login"** (Secondary - White with border) → `/login`

### Footer CTA Section
**Before:** Single "Try Nutri-Check Now" button
**After:** Two buttons
- **"Patient Portal"** (Primary - White) → `/patient-login`
- **"Medical Staff"** (Secondary - Transparent with border) → `/login`

## Visual Hierarchy

### Primary Actions (Green/Prominent)
- Patient Portal buttons
- Encourages patient self-registration
- More visible and inviting

### Secondary Actions (Gray/Subtle)
- Medical Staff login
- Less prominent but still accessible
- Clear separation from patient portal

## User Experience

### For Patients
1. See "Patient Portal" prominently displayed
2. Click to go to patient login page
3. Can self-register with email/password
4. Access personal health dashboard

### For Medical Professionals
1. See "Staff" or "Medical Staff" button
2. Click to go to medical professional login
3. Sign in with admin-created account
4. Access medical professional dashboard

## Key Points

✅ **Clear Separation**: Two distinct portals clearly labeled
✅ **Patient-Friendly**: Patient portal is primary/prominent
✅ **Professional Access**: Medical staff login still accessible
✅ **No Confusion**: Different buttons for different users
✅ **Self-Registration**: Patients can create accounts freely
✅ **Admin Control**: Medical staff accounts still admin-created

## Button Styling

### Patient Portal Buttons
- Green gradient background
- White text
- Prominent shadow
- Hover: Darker green, lifts up

### Medical Staff Buttons
- White/transparent background
- Green/gray text
- Border outline
- Hover: Fills with color

## Responsive Design
- Buttons stack on mobile
- Maintain clear hierarchy
- Touch-friendly sizes
- Proper spacing

## Summary

The landing page now clearly presents **two separate portals**:
1. **Patient Portal** - Self-registration, personal health tracking
2. **Medical Staff** - Admin-created accounts, manage all patients

No more confusion about who can create accounts!
